Coaching & Communication Toolkit

Employee Warning Memo

This memo serves as a formal warning to an employee for a policy violation, such as unauthorized absenteeism. It documents the violation, outlines expectations for improvement, and sets a timeline for monitoring.

Sample Helpdesk Ticket Response

This demonstrates a professional and empathetic approach to customer support. It acknowledges the user's frustration, confirms initial troubleshooting steps, and communicates the next steps, such as escalating to a higher tier of support. It also assures the user of a follow-up to a complete resolution.

Customer Service & Incident Toolkit

Customer Service Standards Statement

This statement emphasizes the importance of respecting customers, acting promptly and professionally, and treating end-users as interlocutors, not just support tickets. It advocates for proactive support, clear communication without jargon, and consistent service delivery.

Escalation Matrix

This matrix defines the different tiers of support (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3, and Incident Coordinator) and outlines the types of issues handled, the response time, and the criteria for escalating tickets.

Sample Helpdesk Ticket Response

A ticket response that shows empathy and a clear plan of action, including escalation when necessary.
